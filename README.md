# 📊 Target E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of the **Target E-Commerce Sales Dataset** using **Python**. The objective is to extract meaningful business insights by analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, seller performance, payment methods, and delivery efficiency.

The project covers the complete data analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI analysis, data visualization, and business recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate seller performance.
- Analyze payment methods and delivery performance.
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Revenue showed strong growth from 2017 to 2018.
- São Paulo contributed the highest revenue.
- Health Beauty and Watches Present were the top revenue-generating categories.
- Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
- Most orders were delivered on time, indicating efficient logistics.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing on high-performing states and cities.
- Increase inventory for top-selling product categories.
- Build loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Improve delivery efficiency in slow-performing regions.
- Optimize shipping costs for remote locations.
